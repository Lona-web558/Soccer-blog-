Argentina win Copa America 2024

Posted on July 15 2024 by Lona Matshingana

Argentina have won the copa America against Colombia, held at Miami at Hard Rock Stadium.They won won 1-0. Lautaro Martinez scored the goal on the 112th minute.
Their win against Colombia makes them the first nation to ever win 2 copa Americas with a World cup in between them.

Manchester United Lose Friendly

Manchester United have lost their friendly match against Rosenborg Bk.
They lost 1-0. This after a difficult game, where they had 5 goals saved. And few corner kicks and few goals on target.
