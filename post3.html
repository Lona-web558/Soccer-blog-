Spain win Euro 2024

Posted on July 14 2024 by Lona Matshingana

Spain have beaten England in the Euro 2024 final. The final took place on Sunday 14 July 2024 in Berlin.
Nico Williams and Mikel Oyarzabal both scored in this incredible final. Spain have now won four tournament games, breaking a tie with Germany for the most in History.

Uruguay win 3rd place in Copa America

Uruguay played against Canada for a 3rd place spot in the Copa America. The game ended 2 all.
And went to penalties, were Uruguay won 4-3 for a 3rd place spot.
